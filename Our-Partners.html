Why Our Partners Matter

At KM Textiles, our partners are not just suppliers — they are an extension of our commitment to excellence. Each partner, whether a machinery manufacturer or logistics provider, has been carefully selected for their track record of quality, reliability, and innovation. Here’s how our partnerships benefit you:
 
Uncompromising Quality: From weaving to finishing, we provide top-tier machinery from Europe’s leading manufacturers.
  
Seamless Logistics: With global logistics leaders handling your shipments, you can rest assured your machinery will arrive on time and in perfect condition.
  
Compliance and Transparency: Our partners ensure that every shipment meets all required customs and import regulations, providing you with peace of mind.
  
Long-Term Support: Beyond delivery, our partnerships ensure that you receive ongoing support, from spare parts to technical expertise, to keep your production running smoothly.

MACHINES SHIPPED

Picanol

Pioneers in weaving machinery technology, based in Belgium
Picanol is one of the leading names in the world of weaving machines. Known for their innovation, precision, and durability, Picanol machines are used in a wide range of textile applications. As an authorized distributor, we offer access to Picanol’s state-of-the-art Jacquard and Rapier weaving machines, engineered for high performance and efficiency.


Specialty: Weaving systems (Jacquard & Rapier)
Key Benefit: High production speeds with energy-efficient designs.
Industry Use: Apparel, technical textiles, home textiles.

Saurer

Swiss engineering at its finest, specializing in spinning and twisting systems
Saurer is a global leader in textile machinery, offering a complete portfolio for spinning and twisting solutions. Their machinery is designed to optimize productivity, minimize waste, and reduce energy consumption. KM Textiles is proud to represent Saurer’s innovative machinery solutions for the North American market.
 
Specialty: Spinning & twisting solutions
Key Benefit: Superior automation and precision.
Industry Use: Cotton, wool, synthetic fibers.

Brückner

Leaders in textile finishing technology, based in Germany
Brückner provides innovative and energy-efficient machines for the finishing of woven and knitted fabrics. From heat-setting and curing to dyeing and printing, Brückner’s solutions are designed to meet the highest standards of quality. Their machines are a perfect fit for those looking to enhance their textile production processes.
 
Specialty: Textile finishing systems
Key Benefit: Cutting-edge energy-saving technology.
Industry Use: Garment finishing, technical textiles, automotive fabrics.

Benninger

Swiss experts in dyeing and finishing machinery
Benninger has been a leader in the development of textile finishing equipment for over 100 years. Their advanced dyeing and coating systems allow for precise color matching and excellent fabric durability. With a focus on sustainable production, Benninger’s machinery helps reduce energy consumption and improve process efficiency.
 
Specialty: Dyeing and finishing machinery
Key Benefit: Eco-friendly technology and process optimization.
Industry Use: Home textiles, industrial fabrics, and sportswear.
