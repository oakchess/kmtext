Who We Are

Rooted in Europe. Focused on the U.S. Market.

KM Textiles was founded to bridge Europe’s textile-machinery expertise with the growing demand of North American manufacturers.

Based in and supported by a logistics hub within the EU, we partner with select European producers to supply high-grade weaving, spinning, dyeing, and finishing equipment to U.S. buyers — without the headaches of direct importation. Our hybrid presence — European headquarters + U.S. field representative — means you get the best of both worlds: European sourcing precision and American-style responsiveness.

Our Story

Founded in Europe, KM Textiles was created to bridge the gap between Europe’s leading textile machinery brands and the growing demand for high-quality production equipment in the U.S. market. With decades of combined experience in textile distribution, engineering, and logistics, we provide American manufacturers with access to European precision, innovation, and reliability. From our first shipment to today, our goal has remained the same: to connect top-tier European machinery with U.S. manufacturers, ensuring every delivery is seamless and every client supported.

Our Values

  • Integrity: Transparent dealings and clear communication with every client and partner.
  • Reliability: Machinery arrives as promised, backed by expert handling and technical knowledge.
  • Excellence: We continually seek to improve our processes, partner network, and client support.
  • Sustainability: Promoting energy-efficient machinery and responsible logistics wherever possible.
